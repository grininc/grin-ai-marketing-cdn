The Platform

Before we ask about pricing, we want you to fully understand what this platform does. Please read through each section carefully.

The problem it solves

Running a creator program at scale means hiring coordinators to do repetitive work: vetting creators one by one, chasing shipments and follow-ups, pulling reports that show numbers but don't explain what happened. As your program grows, you add headcount — but the work stays manual and the insights stay shallow.

This platform replaces 2–3 coordinators with AI that doesn't just automate tasks — it makes intelligent decisions. It scores creators, detects churn risk, reallocates budgets, drafts briefs, and generates reports that explain why results changed, not just what the numbers are.

What it includes

Core operations (included in every subscription): Automated outreach sequences, shipment tracking, content collection, creator payments, compliance monitoring, CRM with full communication history, and standard reporting.

AI intelligence layer (via optional workflows): Each workflow automates a major program function. The AI performs 5–9 discrete tasks per creator — scoring, analysis, optimization, and reporting. You choose which workflows to activate based on what your program needs.

AI chat assistant: Ask any question about your program — "Which creators drove the most revenue last quarter?" or "What should I do about declining engagement?" — and get an instant answer grounded in your data. Included with the platform, no usage caps.

Component 2: Active Creator Fee
What it covers: For every creator who is actively engaged in your program — they posted content, clicked a link, converted, or received a gift — you pay a small per-creator fee. This covers the platform storing their data, tracking their activity, managing communications, processing payments, and running compliance checks.

Important details:
• Prospective creators are free — you only pay once a creator actively engages
• Volume discounts apply: the rate drops as your program scales (e.g., from $2.00 at 200 creators down to $1.25 at 1,000+)
• Annual commitment locks your rate — your discount is guaranteed for the year

What it replaces: The per-creator overhead your team incurs managing each relationship — tracking activity, logging communications, processing payments, and monitoring compliance. At scale, this work adds up to significant coordination cost per creator.
